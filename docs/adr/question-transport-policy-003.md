# ADR — question-transport-policy-003

### ADR-003: Mold keeps structured A/B/C/D forks  [status: accepted]

- **Context:** The maximal #294 reading would migrate mold to culture's prose-only fork model, gutting mold's structured-dialogue identity.
- **Decision:** Mold keeps tiered lettered options, gains the transport pointer (#287 fix) plus a conformance line: a structured fork is valid only when its depth was contributed in-dialogue first.
- **Alternatives:** Anti-bundling cap for mold (rejected: bounds-round batching of independent scoping questions is by design); prose-only migration (rejected: culture's tightening is per-skill, not the shared policy).
- **Consequences:** Mold and culture legitimately differ on top of one shared rule; the conformance line is the audit hook.
